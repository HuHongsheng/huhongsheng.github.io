  • I am not a member of the Communist Party of China. But my grandfather is, and he has been a member for over 50 years. Someone sympathized his low pension. My grandfather said, "The people have given me too much. What have I done for the people? It is not worth that much." He is 90 years old currently, and living a healthy and happy life. Recently he learned how to use WeChat (Oct., 2023).
    我不是中共党员. 但我爷爷是, 而且入党已经超过了 50 年. 曾有人同情他的退休金过少. 我爷爷说: "人民给我这些已经太多了, 我为人民做了什么? 根本值不了这么多钱." 他 90 岁了, 目前身体健康, 心情愉快, 最近学会了如何使用微信 (2023 年 10 月).
